Cardiac Ultrasonography (SIEMENS ACUSON SC2000)

  • Instantaneous full-volume imaging. The ACUSON SC2000 ultrasound system introduces a new paradigm in cardiac imaging. Unlike traditional 3D ultra sound systems that require four to seven heart cycles to stitch together a volume, the ACUSON SC2000 volume imaging ultrasound system delivers true Echo in a Heartbeat-instantaneous full-volume acquisition.
  • Compact, portable, easy to use and upgraded system enables stress-free, speedy procedures.
  • Further enhancing image resolution, received information is combined from multiple images to synthesize the transmit focus through the entire range. Volume imaging ultrasound catheter is enabled on the ACUSON SC2000system to support intracardiac echocardiography. ACUSON SC2000 system images are DICOM 3D-compliant and interchangeable for use on other DICOM 3D-compliant image management platforms.
