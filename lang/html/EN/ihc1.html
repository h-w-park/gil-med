International Healthcare Center

Gil International Healthcare Center (GIHC) does its best to help foreign patients take full advantage of our health care services. GIHC is a reliable link to the Gil Medical Center for patients from overseas. We offer you following services so that foreign patients can receive the very best care during their stay at GIHC.

We do.

  • Review the medical case and make an arrangement for the medical treatment plan via e-mail, fax or phone before visiting the GIHC.
  • Assist with obtaining visa, airport transportation (pick-up service), and lodging.
  • Support your hospitalization, operation, recovery and examination after discharge.
  • Language assistance - English, Russian, Mongolian, Chinese and Japanese.

Specialists of various departments and all the staffs at GIHC will fully support you with the best care and the detailed explanation for your medical case. With excellent medical technology, GIHC provides you with the advanced health care service. Also considering the cultural difference, GIHC has been offering personalized and coordinated care for every patient that has chosen us.
