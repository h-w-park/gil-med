“I Have Korean National Health Insurance.”

If the patient is covered by the Korean National Health Insurance either by employee insurance or local insurance, the patient would pay the same amount of money as Koreans do.

“I Don’t Have any Medical Insurance.”

If you do not have any medical insurance, we recommend you to contact international insurance providers, such as National Health Insurance Corporation, SOS International, GAP and etc. For more information, please visit below websites :

http://internationalsos.com
http://globalassistance.co.kr
http://www.nhic.or.kr/static/html/wbd/g/a/wbdga0101.html
